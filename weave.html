Weaving the Future

Weaving the Future is a print made with from etching on a linoleum block and weaving in fabric to create a multi-dimensional print. This work draws inspiration from weaving as both a cultural technology and a metaphor. Textile practices have long been used by different communities as a means of storytelling, resistance, and survival. In my print, weaving functions symbolically, threads from different histories and identities connect across time and space to form a unified vision of a more inclusive future. This piece also combines Afrofuturism and intersectionality to explore the ways diverse cultures and technologies can be interwoven to imagine new shared futures. My piece depicts a futuristic quilt that travels through multiple different weaving tools, where patterns, symbols, and textures from different cultural traditions come together to create a layered visual narrative.
